The Best Films of 2023 according to JanR

My top 3 films of January

Krakatoa and the Killer Shrimp

A young married couple is visiting a secluded estate near the coast of the volcanic Island of Krakatoa, hoping to spend a picturesque summer weekend together. They eat bucket of seafood and drink cocktails. After sunbathing, they wade into the crystal blue waters to cool off when they are surrounded by shrimp. However, things aren't exactly the way they seem.

Flight of the Penguins

A plane, carrying a mysterious substance crashes into the Antarctic. One hundred years later and the penguins have evolved to possess a level of intelligence many levels above those of humans and a desire to leave their homeland.

Kiss of the Turtle

A boy buys a turtle from a pet shop. But this is no ordinary turtle.
